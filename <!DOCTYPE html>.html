Welcome to Shanhe University

Shanhe University is not a traditional university, but an internet virtual university established by the people of Shanhe's four provinces (Hebei, Henan, Shandong, Shanxi) according to the situation of social education. It is a collective will and aspiration formed by students from these provinces, originating in June 2023. This school was established due to the lack of higher education resources in Hebei, Henan, Shandong, and Shanxi provinces.

The Significance of Our Project

  • Alleviating the shortage of higher education resources: Our project can provide more learning opportunities and resources for students in these areas by using the internet virtual university, which can alleviate the problem of shortage of higher education resources.
  • Promoting the development of educational informatization: The internet virtual university is an important form of educational informatization development. The implementation of our project can promote the development of educational informatization, drive the transformation of teaching methods, and lay the foundation for future education development.
  • Cultivating talent and promoting regional economic development: Through our project, we can cultivate more high-quality talents for the four provinces of Shanhe, enhance the competitiveness of regional talents, and contribute to the economic development and social progress of the region.
  • Exploring new educational models: Our project is a new type of educational model, which breaks the time and space restrictions of traditional education through the internet virtual university. It can explore more flexible ways and modes of education, and provide references and insights for the future development of education.
